INTRODUCTION

Stanislav M. Nikov

I am currently a member of CareerFoundry's community.CareerFoundry is a place where you can learn from the best and aim to prosper in a highly advanced and innovative environment. I am also trying to mine cryptocurrencies in my spare time. I use HiveOS to manage my only rig, but of course - if you have more than one, it will serve you better.
Let's get back on topic! Alas, I started my Web Dev journey on my own and I still don't have the luxury of being friends with the professionals who usually work in teams, because there are so many tasks involved in the process of creating a website and a lot of them are very unique. I didn't imagine, for example, that I'd have to be so aware of and skillful with the visual arts and all the graphic designs that take place. Thankfully, there are tools like Canva and SVGAtor which can really help out a beginner! They are easy to use and one can find plenty of tutorials all over the web, if necessary.
